Our Story

From Hive to Home,
With Love

Our journey began in 2025 in the lush forests of Western Ghats. What started as a passion for beekeeping has grown into a mission to bring the purest, most authentic honey to your table.

We work directly with local beekeepers who practice sustainable harvesting methods. Our honey is never heated, filtered minimally, and packed within 24 hours of extraction to preserve all natural enzymes and nutrients.
